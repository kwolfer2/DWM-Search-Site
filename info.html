What is this?


Dragon Warrior Monsters


Dragon Warrior Monsters is a classic monster-collecting and battling RPG released in 1998 (localized in 2000) for the Nintendo Game Boy. While often compared to Pokémon, this game carves out its own identity with deep breeding mechanics, strategic combat, and an expansive fantasy world rooted in the Dragon Quest universe. Featuring iconic character and creature designs by Akira Toriyama and developed by Enix, Dragon Warrior Monsters lets players train, breed, and command an army of monsters in their quest to become the ultimate Monster Master.


How do I use this site and what makes it work?


The point of this site is to provide information on the mosnters found in the game, commonly called a Beastiary.


Monster Search

This page serves as the primary way to access the raw info on each monster and features a stat projection feature. Monsters in Dragon Warrior Monsters do not have static assigned stats, they have growth rates. This means that the stats are any given monster are entirely dependent on a variety of factors that essentially boil down to Starting Stat Values + (Growth Rates * Current Level) = Current Stat Values
